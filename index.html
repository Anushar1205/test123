How to Scan and Monitor:

  • GA4: Use the **Google Tag Assistant** Chrome/Firefox extension.
  • Meta: Use the **Meta Pixel Helper** Chrome/Firefox extension.
  • LinkedIn: Use the **LinkedIn Insight Tag Helper** Chrome/Firefox extension.
  • You can also check the **Network tab** in your browser's Developer Tools (F12) and filter for requests to `google-analytics`, `facebook`, and `licdn` to see the trackers firing.
